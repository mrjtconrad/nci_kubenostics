About this Project

Purpose

The goal of my project is to provide the user with a software application that will enable them to quickly & easily gather relevant diagnostic data from their kubernetes cluster. The application will assist users in auditing their cluster, allowing them to plan their methodology and process of securing the cluster whilst also allowing them to troubleshoot the cluster in the event that a suspected or confirmed cyber security incident has occurred. The features of the project lend themselves to referencing a number of different stages in the NIST Cybersecurity Framework, including Identify, Protect, Detect & Respond.

Scope

My elective stream is Cyber Security. From my year studying with NCI I have developed a strong foundational understanding of what is important when attempting to achieve a good cyber security posture. My takeaway is that it is imperative to firstly understand what you have to secure i.e. an audit of assets. Secondly, the user should be able to assess the security risk to those assets based on their configuration. Armed with all of that information the user can then embark on the cyclical process of hardening & securing their assets. For the scope of this project, the asset will be a kubernetes cluster hosting multiple applications and microservices. I chose this as I feel it has a very modern & industry relevant application.
